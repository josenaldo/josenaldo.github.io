## 1. Elevator Pitch

A lightweight content project that turns a simple recipe into a shareable web experience with strong personal branding.

## 2. Problem and Context

The goal was to publish straightforward content in a web-native format with minimal maintenance cost and high shareability.

## 3. Scope and Role

- Project ideation and concept.
- Content structuring and publishing.
- GitHub-based maintenance and static deployment.

## 4. Solution Implemented

A concise page with clear instructions and a simple reading flow optimized for quick consumption.

## 5. Stack and Architecture

- Static content (HTML/Markdown).
- Hosting via GitHub Pages.

## 6. Quality and Engineering Practices

- Simple navigation.
- Direct, reader-first content style.
- Open licensing strategy (CC0-1.0 in repository).

## 7. Portfolio Relevance

Shows versatility in shipping small but complete web products quickly and effectively.
