# Project Dossier Template (Portfolio)

Use this structure to present projects with market-level clarity.

## 1. Elevator Pitch

One paragraph: what the project is, who it serves, and why it matters.

## 2. Problem and Context

- What pain/problem existed?
- What business or technical scenario motivated the work?

## 3. Scope and Role

- What was your ownership (architecture, backend, frontend, CI/CD, leadership)?
- Team context and responsibilities.

## 4. Solution Overview

- Main approach.
- Trade-offs and constraints considered.

## 5. Architecture and Stack

- Languages/frameworks.
- Data/message infrastructure.
- Patterns (Clean, Hexagonal, DDD, EDA, etc.).

## 6. Quality and Engineering Practices

- Testing strategy.
- Documentation, ADRs, coding standards.
- CI/CD, observability, automation.

## 7. Results and Relevance

- Outcomes/impact (if measurable).
- What this project proves about your seniority.

## 8. Links

- Repository.
- Production/demo.
- Docs/API references.

## 9. Visual Evidence

- One or two screenshots (production and/or repository view).
